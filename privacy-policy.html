Privacy Policy

Your mental health data is sacred. Here's exactly how we protect it.

Last updated: January 2026. We update this whenever our practices change.

1. What data we collect

When you use Where's My Therapist, we collect:

  • Account data: Name, email, phone number, payment information
  • Clinical data: Your conversations with our AI, therapy session notes, medication records, mood check-ins, and health history you share with us
  • Usage data: How you interact with the app (features used, time spent, errors encountered)
  • Device data: Device type, OS version, language preferences, approximate location (country/state only)
  • Payment data: Transaction history, payment method type (we never see your full card details — that goes straight to encrypted payment processors)

2. Why we collect it

We collect your data for one reason: to provide you the best mental healthcare possible, and to keep you safe.

  • Clinical data helps your therapist understand your history and provide continuity of care
  • Usage data helps us improve the app and identify bugs
  • Payment data is needed to process your subscription and ensure you have uninterrupted access
  • Device data helps us optimize performance in low-connectivity areas

We never use your data for advertising, selling, or marketing. Ever.

3. How we protect it

Mental health data is the most sensitive data about you. Here's how we protect it:

  • Encryption in transit: All data moving between your device and our servers is encrypted with TLS 1.3
  • Encryption at rest: All data stored on our servers is encrypted with AES-256
  • End-to-end encryption: Messages between you and your therapist are encrypted on your device and decrypted only by the intended recipient
  • Access controls: Only authorized clinical and support staff can access your data, and only what they need to help you
  • Audit logs: We log every access to your data. We can see who accessed what, when, and why
  • Regular security audits: We conduct third-party security audits annually and penetration testing quarterly
  • Data minimization: We only collect what we need. We delete it when you delete your account

4. Who can access your data

  • Your therapist or psychiatrist: Sees all your clinical data — that's the whole point
  • Our clinical operations team: Can see anonymized data to manage quality and safety
  • Our support team: Can see your account info and conversation history only if you reach out and explicitly ask for help
  • Our engineers: Can see anonymized usage data to improve the app. They cannot see your clinical data
  • Nobody else. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties for marketing. Period.

5. Data retention

  • While you're a user: We keep all your data for clinical continuity and to comply with Indian medical records laws
  • After you delete your account: We delete your clinical data within 30 days, except where required by law to retain it (e.g., for tax purposes, 7 years)
  • Backups: We keep encrypted backups for 90 days in case of disaster recovery, then delete them
  • Law enforcement requests: We will never voluntarily share your data with police, government agencies, or anyone else without a valid legal order. If we receive a legal order, we will notify you first (unless legally prohibited)

6. Your rights

Under Indian law (particularly the DPDP Act and Telemedicine Practice Regulations), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all your personal data we hold
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Ask us to delete your data (with limited exceptions for legal compliance)
  • Portability: Get your data in a machine-readable format to move to another service
  • Withdraw consent: You can stop using the app and we'll delete your data (except legally required retention)
  • Object: You can object to specific processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@whereismytherapist.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

7. Children's privacy

Where's My Therapist is not intended for children under 13. If we discover we've collected data from someone under 13, we'll delete it immediately. If you're a parent and believe we have data on your child, please contact privacy@whereismytherapist.com.

8. International data transfers

Your data is stored in data centers within India, compliant with the Data Localization requirements of the DPDP Act. We do not transfer data internationally without explicit consent.

9. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Payment processors: Razorpay, Pine Labs (for UPI, cards, net banking) — your payment data goes directly to them, encrypted
  • Cloud hosting: AWS (India region only) — subject to our data encryption and access controls
  • Video calling: Jio Meet (for secure encrypted therapy sessions)
  • Analytics: Plausible (privacy-first analytics — no tracking cookies, no personal data stored)

All third-party services are contractually obligated to protect your data with the same standards we do.

10. Cookies and tracking

We use minimal cookies:

  • Session cookies (required for login and app functionality)
  • Preference cookies (remember your language choice, dark mode preference)

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or cross-site tracking. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work.

11. Changes to this policy

We'll update this policy if our practices change. We'll notify you by email if we make material changes (like selling your data or changing encryption practices). We'll never make your data less protected — only more protected or equally protected.

12. Contact us

Questions about this policy? Reach out:

  • Founder: founder@whereismytherapist.com
  • Support: support@whereismytherapist.com

13. Regulatory compliance

We comply with:

  • Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023
  • Telemedicine Practice Guidelines (2020) — Medical Council of India
  • Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011
  • Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023 (BNS)
🔒 Our Promise

Your mental health data will never be sold. It will never be shared with advertisers. It will never be used against you. We built this platform because we care about your privacy. That's non-negotiable.
