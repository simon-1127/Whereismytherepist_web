Frequently asked questions

How long does it take to hear back?
We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours, usually much faster. For urgent matters, please call our support line.
Can I request a feature or report a bug?
Absolutely! We love hearing from users. Please include as much detail as possible, and we'll review it with the product team.
Do you offer partnerships or integrations?
Yes! We're open to strategic partnerships with employers, insurers, and other health platforms. Reach out to hello@whereismytherapist.com with details.
How do I apply for a job?
Head over to our Careers page to see open roles and apply directly.
Can I partner for research or clinical studies?
We're very interested in advancing mental health research. Please email partnerships@whereismytherapist.com with your proposal.
