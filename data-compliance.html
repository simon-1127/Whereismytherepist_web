Data Compliance

We comply with every Indian regulation that protects your mental health data. Here's how.

Last updated: January 2026. This document explains our compliance framework.

We comply with

Where's My Therapist complies with the following Indian laws and regulations:

1. Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023

India's primary data protection law. We meet all requirements:

  • Consent: We obtain explicit consent before collecting personal and sensitive personal data
  • Purpose limitation: We use data only for the stated purposes (providing mental healthcare)
  • Data minimization: We collect only what's necessary
  • Accuracy: We maintain accurate, updated data
  • Security: We implement appropriate safeguards (encryption, access controls, audit logs)
  • Retention: We delete data when no longer needed (within 30 days of account deletion)
  • User rights: We honor access, correction, deletion, and portability requests
  • Breach notification: We notify you and authorities of data breaches within 72 hours

2. Telemedicine Practice Guidelines, 2020 (Medical Council of India)

Governs how we deliver remote mental healthcare:

  • Licensed providers: All therapists and psychiatrists must be licensed with the Medical Council
  • Patient consent: Patients must explicitly agree to telemedicine consultation
  • Records maintenance: We maintain complete clinical records for at least 3 years
  • Confidentiality: All communications are confidential and encrypted
  • Informed refusal: Patients can refuse telemedicine and request in-person care
  • Emergency protocols: We have procedures to identify and respond to emergencies
  • Referral system: We refer complex cases to appropriate specialists

3. Information Technology Act, 2000 (ITA 2000)

Governs cybersecurity and data protection:

  • Data security: We implement industry-standard security measures (encryption, firewalls, intrusion detection)
  • Computer security incidents: We report security incidents to authorities as required
  • Privacy: We safeguard sensitive personal information (health, financial data)
  • Network security: We maintain secure infrastructure with regular security audits

4. IT Rules 2021 (Information Technology (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Ethics Code) Rules, 2021)

Governs content moderation and user protection:

  • Grievance officer: We have a dedicated grievance redressal officer (complaints@whereismytherapist.com)
  • Community standards: We maintain clear rules against illegal, hateful, or abusive content
  • Prompt removal: We remove illegal content within 36 hours of notification
  • User reporting: Users can report violations through our in-app reporting system
  • Transparency: We publish monthly transparency reports on content moderation

5. Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS), 2023

India's new criminal code with provisions for privacy and cybercrime:

  • Privacy violations: We do not breach user privacy or violate confidentiality
  • Cybercrime: We implement measures to prevent hacking, data theft, and unauthorized access
  • Harmful content: We remove content that violates BNS provisions

6. Drugs and Cosmetics Act, 1940 (for psychiatry services)

Governs pharmaceutical prescriptions:

  • Licensed psychiatrists: Only registered medical practitioners can prescribe medications
  • Proper evaluation: Psychiatrists conduct thorough evaluations before prescribing
  • Monitoring: We track medication adherence through MediTrack feature
  • Restricted substances: We follow all restrictions on controlled substances

7. Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016

Ensures accessibility for users with disabilities:

  • Accessibility: We support multiple languages, screen readers, and accessibility standards (WCAG 2.1 AA)
  • Inclusive design: We design for users with diverse abilities
  • Reasonable accommodation: We provide reasonable accommodations upon request

Data localization compliance

Under the DPDP Act and other regulations, critical personal data must be stored in India:

  • ✓ All personal and health data is stored in AWS India (Mumbai) data centers
  • ✓ We do not transfer sensitive data outside India without explicit consent
  • ✓ Backups are encrypted and stored within India
  • ✓ Payment processing is handled by RBI-authorized payment service providers

Healthcare compliance

  • Medical registration: All therapists and psychiatrists are registered with relevant medical councils
  • Insurance compliance: Our services can be claimed under most health insurance policies
  • GST compliance: We comply with all tax regulations. Services include applicable GST
  • Telemedicine prescribing: We follow RBI guidelines for video consultations with psychiatrists

Security certifications and audits

  • Third-party security audits: Conducted annually by independent security firms
  • Penetration testing: Quarterly testing by certified ethical hackers
  • Vulnerability assessments: Monthly scanning and remediation
  • Compliance certification: ISO 27001 (Information Security Management) compliance roadmap
  • HIPAA-equivalent standards: We follow healthcare data protection standards equivalent to HIPAA

Data breach response protocol

In case of a data breach, we will:

  1. Investigate immediately and contain the breach
  2. Notify affected users within 72 hours
  3. Report to authorities (MEITY, DPA, if applicable)
  4. Provide credit monitoring and remediation support
  5. Conduct post-incident review and improve security
  6. Maintain transparency throughout the process

User rights and remedies

Under Indian law, you have the right to:

  • File complaints: To the Data Protection Authority (once established)
  • Seek damages: For violations of the DPDP Act
  • Criminal prosecution: For severe privacy violations
  • Consumer redressal: Through consumer protection courts
  • Grievance with us: Email grievances@whereismytherapist.com for resolution

Regular compliance audits

We conduct regular compliance checks on:

  • Data collection, use, and retention practices
  • User consent and rights fulfillment
  • Security measures and incident response
  • Provider licensing and qualifications
  • Content moderation and community guidelines
  • Telemedicine compliance

International standards we follow

While India-centric, we also follow international best practices:

  • GDPR alignment: We apply GDPR principles to all users for data minimization and transparency
  • ISO 27001: Information security management standards
  • NIST Cybersecurity Framework: Critical infrastructure protection best practices
  • OWASP Top 10: Protection against common web application vulnerabilities
