Building mental healthcare that actually works for India

We're a team of therapists, engineers, and advocates who believe mental health shouldn't be a luxury — it should be accessible, affordable, and in your language.

Our Mission

197 million Indians need mental healthcare. 48 million get it. Of those, most have to pay out-of-pocket because insurance barely covers it. And almost all of it is in English — a language only 10% of India speaks fluently at home.

We exist to fix this. We built an all-in-one platform where you get an AI companion you name yourself (there 24/7), a licensed therapist (for real clinical care), and a community that gets it — all in 10 Indian languages, paid with the payment methods you already use, at prices that don't require you to earn a Silicon Valley salary.

Every feature we build. Every decision we make. Starts with one question: "Would an Indian family earning ₹20,000/month be able to afford this?"

Our Values

🤝
Equity First
We price for actual Indian incomes. We support 10 languages. We accept UPI, not just credit cards. There's no "economy tier" where you get less care — everyone gets the same clinical rigor.
🔬
Evidence-Backed
Our AI is trained on CBT, DBT, and ACT. Our therapists are licensed. Our features are designed with clinical input, not marketing noise. We measure outcomes because mental health is serious.
🌱
Destigmatizing
Mental illness is an illness. Full stop. We talk about it like we talk about diabetes. We build tools that normalize therapy. And we never, ever judge someone for getting help.
🛡️
Privacy Sacred
Your mental health data is the most sensitive data about you. We encrypt end-to-end. We never sell it. We comply with every Indian privacy law. Full stop.

Meet the Team

Chitranshu Simon
Founder & CEO
Why I Built Where's My Therapist I didn't start Where's My Therapist because I saw a business opportunity. I started it because I was looking for help myself. During one of the most difficult periods of my life, I knew I needed therapy. Taking that first step was already hard—but finding the right therapist felt even harder. I spent hours searching through websites, comparing profiles, reading reviews, and trying to understand who would actually be the right fit. Then came another reality check: the cost. Many therapy sessions were priced far beyond what I could comfortably afford. And I kept wondering how many people, just like me, give up—not because they don't want help, but because they can't find someone they trust within their budget. That's when one question changed everything: "Why is getting mental health support so complicated when it's needed the most?" The more I spoke with friends and others, the more I realized my experience wasn't unique. So many people were struggling to find the right therapist, compare prices, understand specialties, and book with confidence. That's why I built Where's My Therapist. Not just another therapist directory—but a platform designed around the people seeking help. Our mission is to make therapy more accessible, transparent, and affordable by helping you: - Find therapists based on your unique needs, not just location. - Compare pricing upfront—no hidden surprises. - Discover professionals who fit both your emotional needs and your budget. - Spend less time searching and more time healing. Everyone deserves access to quality mental healthcare, regardless of their financial situation. If Where's My Therapist can help even one person avoid the confusion, stress, and financial uncertainty that I experienced, then this journey will have been worth it. Thank you for believing in this mission and being here from the beginning. Together, we're building a future where finding a therapist feels hopeful—not overwhelming. — Founder, Where's My Therapist
